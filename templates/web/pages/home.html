About Forever Living Products Company

Forever Living is the world largest aloe vera company in the world. Forever was founded in 1978 here in Phoenix, Arizona by a young gentleman called Rex Maughan. Together with experts on aloe Rex found a way to harvest and stabilize aloe vera which was one of the key ingredients to success of Forever Living. Imagine that you can suddenly have a product available across the world that is practically identical to a fresh cut aloe vera leaf - unbelievable. Not only in United States but around the globe.

Then, for a company to thrive for over 42 years there is got to be a very powerful leadership and that's another greatness that Rex invested into - great group of people who worked hard, smart, ethical and proved the world that you can build something great working together.

Finally, true success of Forever Living Products is based on how products are distributed, promoted and provided to clients - which is quite different than a traditional way.

What Products do we have and how to order them?

Forever Living has a range of mostly aloe vera based products. Drinking products where Aloe Vera Gel is the world wide most popular product in Forever company. Following with other natural based drinks, then weight loss products, Bee hive products, Skin Care, Makeup, Nutrition and Supplements, Essential Oils and very fine set of Personal Care Products. Rex's philosophy is that Forever must make only the best products or otherwise product will not be made at all, hence products we have are best of it's kind. Quality is tested, checked, reviewed and approved by many organizations and hence Forever Living Products carry many accreditations, seals and approvals for quality and consistency.
