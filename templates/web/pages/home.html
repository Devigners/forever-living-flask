Forever Living History: Founded in 1978

Forever Living Products Company is an American-based multi-level marketing company that specializes in health and beauty products. Founded in 1978, the company has grown from a small business to one of the world's largest producers of aloe vera and beehive-related products.

The company was founded by CEO Rex Maughan who wanted to build a successful and socially responsible business. He began his career with just two employees in Scottsdale, Arizona, and now employs more than 9,000 people worldwide. Forever Living Products has become one of the top 50 direct-selling companies with its product range spanning 150 countries worldwide. The company focuses on using natural ingredients for its products such as aloe vera and bee pollen to ensure that customers get only the highest quality items.
